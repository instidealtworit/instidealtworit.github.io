ABOUT INSTIDEAL


WHO ARE WE?

Frankly speaking, we are a bunch of enthusiasts who planned to pave a way for a new revolution. We are just normal students like you who have crossed that path you are about to start. Cracking and getting into a premier institute like IITs and AIIMs is no child’s play, but with proper guidance and methods you can succeed. We just try to connect you with the right source without wasting your vital time searching every nook and corner for the best institute.



WHY WE STARTED?

As digitalization is taking the world into a new level and to add upon we can almost do anything just at a mere click. We can order food, study from far flung areas, so why not search for institutes? Many institutes don’t only have the zeal to provide our country with better minds but also all the facilities available. But all they lack is proper exposure. They need to get a platform to showcase their facilities which is the sole motto of our organisation.



OUR PROVISIONS

As we said, we already have crossed that path; we know the hardships hearing about a lot of institutes from every source you can. Finally you will succumb to the confusion. We are here to clear away all your confusions and provide all the institutes on one platform so that you don’t waste money on the wrong place. All you have to do is log in as a member and scroll down your options as per your requirements.
